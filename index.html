EN LA MIRA // EPISODIO 7: ACTO V - VALORANT

WE ARE VALORANT

CHALLENGE THE LIMITS

Combine your style and experience in a global and competitive scenario. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side with precise weapons and tactical skills. Plus, with only one life per round, you'll have to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive. Take on enemies in competitive and normal modes, as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush.

MAPS

SUNSET

A disaster at a local Kingdom facility threatens to bury the entire neighborhood. Visit your favorite food truck and then take the fight to the city on this traditional three-lane map.

LOTUS

A mysterious structure whose walls protect an astral conduit radiating ancestral power. Immense, monolithic doors provide a variety of movement opportunities and open the way to three mysterious sites.

PEARL

Attackers advance on defenders in this two-site map, set in an underwater city. Pearl is a map focused on the environment, without mechanics. Take the battle to a compact Mid or larger areas in our first map on Earth Omega.

FRACTURE

A top-secret research center divided by a failed radianite experiment. With defense options as divided as the map itself, the choice is yours: face the attackers on their own territory or batten down the hatches and resist the assault.

BREEZE

Enjoy views of historic ruins or seaside caves in this tropical paradise. But bring something to cover you from the sun... and long-range shots. You will need it in these large open spaces. Watch your flanks and feel the breeze.

ICEBOX

Your next battlefield is a secret Kingdom excavation site somewhere in the arctic. Both Planting sites are protected by both snow and metal, and are not easily accessible. horizontal. Take advantage of the zip lines and they will never see you coming.

HAVEN

Beneath a forgotten monastery, a clamor emerges between rival agents fighting to control three sites. There is more territory to control, but defenders can use the extra terrain to make aggressive attacks.

SPLIT

If you want to go far, you must go up. A pair of sites divided by a raised center allows for rapid movement by means of two rope elevators. Each site has a large tower vital for control. Remember to look up before everything goes up in the air.

ASCENT

An open terrain for positioning battles and attrition matches divides Ascent into two sites. You can fortify each one using destructible doors: once in place, there will be no way through unless you knock them down. Give up as little territory as possible.
